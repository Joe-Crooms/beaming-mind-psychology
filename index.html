About

Dr. Veronica Medina, PsyD

I am a Florida-licensed clinical psychologist with extensive training in neuropsychological testing and over 12 years of experience diagnosing ADHD, Autism, Learning Disorders, and related conditions.

I specialize in diagnosing neurodevelopmental disorders as well as the conditions that often accompany them — including mood and anxiety disorders — and in helping individuals with these challenges live authentic and fulfilling lives.

I use a variety of widely accepted psychological tests to measure cognitive and academic functioning, and I provide psychotherapy that meets individuals where they are — whether they are new to therapy and looking to learn more about themselves, or coping with a specific challenge in their lives.

I offer psychotherapy in Florida and 43 other states via telehealth.

FAQ

Good to know

What can be diagnosed through testing?

Testing can identify ADHD, Autism, Learning Disorders, and related mood, anxiety, and behavioral conditions, using widely accepted measures of cognitive and academic functioning.

Is testing available via telehealth?

Psychological testing is completed in-office only. Telehealth is available for initial interviews, feedback sessions, and therapy.

Do you accept insurance?

I am out-of-network with all insurances. A superbill can be provided for you to submit to your insurer for possible reimbursement.

What age range do you see?

I evaluate and provide therapy for children, adolescents, and adults, from age 4 through adulthood.
